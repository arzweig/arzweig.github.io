THE GREAT OUTDOORS

Ms. Zweig is a lover of the outdoors. Some of her hobbies include hiking, biking, and taking long walks through hunting territory when it may or may not still be hunting season. She also enjoys photographing nature.

As a lover of nature, Ms. Zweig would like to preserve it. She aspires to one day grow all her own food, tend a few beehives, and revolutionize the agriculture industry by making it more sustainable. Agriculture is currently one of the biggest polluters and contributes significantly to greenhouse gas emissions, and she would like to reduce that impact.
