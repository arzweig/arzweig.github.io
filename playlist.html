MUSIC

If Ariel is at home, she is probably listening to music. She is open to listening to practically any genre of music but generally tends towards folk, indie, rock, R&B or rap. She especially likes to discover new artists by listening to the Rutgers and Princeton radio stations. Through these, she has discovered local bands like Stick Bug, Hong Kong Graffiti, and Hodera. She is looking forward to attending Governor's Ball music festival in June. Read on for an example playlist she would play on her future radio show.
