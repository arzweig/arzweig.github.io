ABOUT ARIEL

Ariel Zweig is a current high school senior and not-soon-enough-to-be high school graduate. A proud East Brunswickian since the age of 3, Ariel is ready to leave for a considerable amount of time, though she is a firm believer in the assertion that (sometimes) NJ Isn't Boring. At this very moment there is a 95% chance she is either listening to music, eating, sleeping, or breathing. Nothing gives her a thrill like the sense of discovery, roaming through quaint towns filled with cute stores, and tom ka gai soup. She can be found in her lavender-walled room reading the New Yorker, listening to 90.3 The Core at a moderate decibel level, and procrastinating anything and everything.

Ms. Zweig hopes to attend any university, host her own radio show, be a writer for the New Yorker, and live on a farm. After retirement, she plans on opening a store that sells paper goods, yarn, plants, and ice cream.
